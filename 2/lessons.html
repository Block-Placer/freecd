AI Learning Lessons

Lesson 1: What is Artificial Intelligence?

AI means machines that can think and learn like humans. It helps computers recognize faces, translate languages, and even drive cars. In this lesson, you’ll learn how AI takes in data, learns patterns, and gives smart answers.

Activity: List 5 examples of AI you see around you every day.

Lesson 2: How Do AI Assistants Work?

AI assistants like Siri, Alexa, and ChatGPT understand words and give answers by learning from large amounts of information. They don’t think like people, but they follow patterns to guess the best reply.

Activity: Write a short question you would ask an AI assistant and think about how it might find the answer.

Lesson 3: The Good and the Bad of AI

AI can make our lives easier by saving time and solving hard problems. But if not used wisely, it can cause problems like fake news, job loss, or privacy risks. This lesson helps you understand both sides of AI.

Activity: Write down one good and one bad use of AI that you can think of.

Lesson 4: The Future of AI

AI is growing every day, from smart robots to driverless cars. In the future, AI will help in hospitals, schools, and even space. Learning how it works will help you use it wisely and safely.

Activity: Imagine one way AI could help make the world a better place and draw it on paper.
