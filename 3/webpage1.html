The History of Phones:

The phone's history stems from Alexander Graham Bell's invention of an electric telephone in 1876, followed by the launch of wired landline services. Mobile communication evolved in the 20th Century from oversized car storages to the first portable cellular phone, made by Motorola in 1973. The technology was further advanced with IBM's first smartphone, which came to market in 1994, with an advanced touchscreen and capable features. The modern age was initiated with Apple's iPhone in 2007, which lead to where mobile computing and mobile smartphones are today - portable powerful computers.
