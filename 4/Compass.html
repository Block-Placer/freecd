Compass

A compass is a tool that helps us find direction. It works by using Earth’s magnetic field to show where north is. People have used it for hundreds of years — sailors, travelers, and hikers — to make sure they don’t lose their way.

These days, compasses are also digital. You can find them in phones, airplanes, and even drones. But the idea is still the same: the needle or sensor lines up with Earth’s magnetic field and points you toward north.

Whether you’re out exploring or just learning about how people navigate, the compass is a simple but smart invention that’s helped humans travel safely for centuries.
